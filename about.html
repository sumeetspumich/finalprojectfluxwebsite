Our Story

Building Michigan's next generation of UX designers through community, education, and hands-on experience.

Our Mission

FLUX was founded in 2021 by a group of passionate Michigan students who saw the need for practical design education that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

Our mission is to empower students with the skills, tools, and confidence they need to succeed in the competitive field of UX/UI design.
