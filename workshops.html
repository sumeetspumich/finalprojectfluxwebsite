Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is needed for our beginner workshops. We'll teach you everything from scratch!

What should I bring?

Just bring your laptop with Figma installed. We'll provide all other necessary materials.

Are workshops recorded?

Yes, all workshops are recorded and available to registered participants for 30 days.

Can I get a refund?

Full refunds are available up to 48 hours before the workshop starts.
